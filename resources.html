Top Resume-Related Questions

1. What should I include in my resume?
Include your contact details, a professional summary, work experience, education, skills, and certifications relevant to the job.
2. How long should a resume be?
A resume should typically be one page, but two pages are acceptable for professionals with extensive experience.
3. How can I make my resume stand out?
Use a clean, modern design, tailor your resume to the job, and highlight measurable achievements and skills.
4. Should I include a photo on my resume?
In most cases, it's unnecessary. However, some industries or countries may prefer a photo.
5. How do I handle gaps in my employment history?
Be honest and focus on skills or experiences gained during the gap, such as volunteering or additional training.
